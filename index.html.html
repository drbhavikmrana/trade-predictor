📖 How To Use — Quick Start Guide NEW USER
▼ EXPAND
STEP 1
🔍
Enter a Ticker
Type any NSE stock symbol in the search box — e.g. SBIN, RELIANCE, TCS — or click any Quick Pick chip below the box.
STEP 2
⚡
Click ANALYZE
Hit the yellow ANALYZE button or press Enter. The app fetches the latest price and computes the Momentum Score, Sector Strength, and Volume Signal.
STEP 3
📊

Check the Execution Signal badge on the top right of the result card. Only act on 💎 ALPHA BUY or ✅ BUY signals.
STEP 4
🎯
Use Entry Levels
Use the Entry Point to buy, Stop Loss to exit if wrong, and Target 1 / Target 2 to book profits.
Signal Legend
💎 ALPHA BUY
Strongest signal. Score ≥80 + Sector Leader + Whale/Institutional volume. Enter with full position.
✅ BUY
Valid trend. Score ≥75, not a laggard, volume is present. Enter with 50–75% of normal position.
⚠️ CAUTION
Mixed signals. Either sector drag or no smart money volume. Wait for better setup.
❌ AVOID
Weak momentum. Score below 50. Stay in cash. No exceptions — do not trade this setup.
🟡 WATCH
Momentum building but not confirmed. Add to watchlist and re-analyze next session.
📊 Score Guide
80–100 High conviction  ·  70–79 Emerging  ·  Below 70 No-trade zone

Pro Tips from Dr. Bhavik Rana
💡 Golden rule: Only trade 💎 ALPHA BUY signals for highest accuracy.
💡 Volume trap: High score + ❄️ LOW VOL = fake breakout. Avoid.
💡 Best time: Analyze between 9:30–10:00 AM after market opens.
💡 Position size: Never risk more than 2% of capital on one trade.
💡 SL discipline: Always place Stop Loss before entering a trade.
